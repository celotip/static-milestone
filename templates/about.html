Vision:

To become the most cherished Indonesian home cooking restaurant, where every meal brings the warmth and authentic flavors of an Indonesian home to our guests, creating memorable dining experiences that celebrate the rich culinary heritage of Indonesia.

Mission:

  1. Authenticity: To provide our customers with an authentic taste of Indonesian home cooking, using traditional recipes and high-quality ingredients.
  2. Hospitality: To create a welcoming and friendly atmosphere where guests feel like they are dining in an Indonesian home, treated with warmth and exceptional service.
  3. Community Engagement: To engage with the local community by sourcing ingredients from local farmers and suppliers, and by participating in community events and activities.
